Education Needed

A two-year degree in video game art can provide you with the background for an entry-level job in video game or multimedia design. You'll need access to a computer with required graphics software to embark on this program. Topics covered in an associate's degree in video art include graphic imaging, 3D computer animation, and character design. A bachelor's program in game design or game art and animation provides a broad background in digital graphics and design useful for obtaining a job as a game artist. Bachelor's degree programs focus on creating a foundation in visual art and graphic design, introducing students to advanced concepts (such as 3D modeling), and instructing individuals on the use of state-of-the-art computer programs. Programs typically include fully realized design projects that can be used as part of a portfolio, which showcases a game artist's abilities and work.
